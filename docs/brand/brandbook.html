Liturgy

A ceremonial identity system built for the edge of land and sea — where fashion, ritual, and biological horror are photographed as forgotten religious painting, submerged for centuries.

001 — EST. TIDAL YEAR
01

Premise

Every frame is a procession. Nothing attacks, nothing performs — the figures are simply being reclaimed, slowly, by an ecosystem older than the camera itself. The horror is ambiguity, not violence. The luxury is restraint, not shine.

RegisterAvant-garde fashion, museum photography, oceanic biology, fine-art horror. Never fantasy. Never cyberpunk.
Emotional keyEerie, ritualistic, funereal, quiet. A held breath rather than a scream.
Governing rule95% black. One light source. Everything else is allowed to disappear.

04

Light & Texture

One source onlyOvercast lighthouse window, skylight in an abandoned cathedral, moonlight diffused through fog. Never a second fill.
Blacks stay blackLarge areas of negative space remain completely unreadable. Do not lift shadows in post.
Highlights on moisture onlySkin catches specular reflections; clothing disappears into darkness. Light exists to reveal wetness, not form.
Grain is structuralCoarse film grain, condensation, salt residue, cracked skin, oxidized metal — nothing is left digitally clean.

The Eight Beats

IThe ArrivalA single figure emerges from absolute darkness. Only the face exists.
IIInitiationThree figures in black water, eyes closed, hair dripping, no movement.
IIICommunionEel-like forms appear at collars and sleeves — unclear if garment or organism.
IVTitheMacro studies: skin, water, scars, salt crystals, wet leather.
VProcessionSix figures arranged as a submerged altarpiece. Negative space dominates.
VISheddingGarments dissolve into kelp and membrane. Human silhouettes begin disappearing.
VIICathedral of WaterThe environment overwhelms the fashion. Architecture barely visible behind mist.
VIIIReturnOne final portrait, nearly swallowed by darkness. Only pale skin and wet reflection remain.
08

Discipline

Hold the line

  • Let 95% of the frame remain unreadable black
  • One light source, always
  • Composition off-center, icon-like, suspended
  • Texture over color as the primary signal
  • Ambiguity between garment and organism

Never allow

  • A second fill light or lifted shadow
  • Saturated color, neon, or warm skin tones
  • Visible logos or futuristic armor
  • Digitally clean, grain-free surfaces
  • Creatures depicted as attacking or threatening
